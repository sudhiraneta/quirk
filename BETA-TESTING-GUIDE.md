# Quirk Chrome Extension - Beta Testing Guide

Welcome to the Quirk beta! This extension analyzes your Pinterest boards and gives you a fun personality roast based on your vibe.

## Installation Instructions

### Step 1: Download the Extension
You should have received a file called `quirk-extension-v1.0.zip`

### Step 2: Unzip the File
1. Locate the `quirk-extension-v1.0.zip` file in your Downloads folder
2. Double-click to unzip it
3. You should now have a folder called `quirk`

### Step 3: Install in Chrome
1. Open Google Chrome
2. Go to `chrome://extensions/` (copy and paste this into your address bar)
3. Enable **Developer mode** by toggling the switch in the top-right corner
4. Click the **Load unpacked** button
5. Select the `quirk` folder you unzipped in Step 2
6. The Quirk extension should now appear in your extensions list!

### Step 4: Pin the Extension (Optional but Recommended)
1. Click the puzzle piece icon in your Chrome toolbar (top-right)
2. Find "Quirk" in the list
3. Click the pin icon next to it
4. The Quirk icon will now appear in your toolbar

## How to Use Quirk

### Step 1: Go to Pinterest
1. Navigate to [pinterest.com](https://pinterest.com)
2. Make sure you're logged in
3. Browse your feed or go to your profile to see your saved pins

### Step 2: Activate Quirk
1. Click the Quirk extension icon in your Chrome toolbar
2. Click the **Analyze My Vibe** button
3. Wait a few seconds while Quirk analyzes your pins

### Step 3: Get Your Results!
You'll see:
- Your dominant personality type
- A personalized roast
- A breakdown of your vibe percentages
- Total number of pins analyzed

## Personality Types

Quirk can detect these personality vibes:

- **Tech Minimalist Queen** - Clean, modern, productivity-focused aesthetic
- **Pilates Princess Energy** - Wellness, beach, soft aesthetic vibes
- **Chaotic Food Curator** - Recipe obsessed, foodie lifestyle
- **Professional Vibe Curator** - European summer, coffee shop aesthetic
- **The Balanced Baddie** - A mix of everything!

## Troubleshooting

### "Please open Pinterest first!"
Make sure you're on pinterest.com before clicking Analyze

### "Please refresh Pinterest and try again"
1. Refresh the Pinterest page
2. Make sure you're logged in and can see pins
3. Scroll down to load some pins
4. Try again

### "No pins found"
- Scroll down on Pinterest to load more pins
- Make sure images are fully loaded
- Try going to your profile or a specific board

## Privacy

Quirk only analyzes pins visible on your current Pinterest page. Nothing is saved or sent anywhere - all analysis happens locally in your browser.
